H e l l o !




Hanyu is an experience designer who believes that design is an endless journey to "better solutions". She doesn't like to be defined by titles, because definitions of everything would evolve as society moves forward. However the format of interface changes, design is the key to make technologies accessible and serve people better.

Her insterests in designing at the intersection of software and hardware, online and offline, technology and human are rooted in her education background: She got her master degree in Human-Computer Interaction from Georgia Tech. Before that, she spent her undergrad years studying Communications (Advertising) and Industrial Design.

When she's not designing, you can find her going to theaters for musicals & dramas, reading history, sociology & science fictions.

Also, she loves documenting when she's "on the road".
