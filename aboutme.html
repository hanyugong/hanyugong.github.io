H e l l o !



Hanyu is an experience designer who believes that design is an endless journey to "better solutions". She is now pursuing a Masters degree in Human-Computer Interaction at Georgia Tech. Before moving to Atlanta, she studied in both Communications (Advertising) and Industrial Design.

Hanyu doesn't like to be defined by titles. She believes definitions of everything would evolve as society moves forward. Therefore, she likes to break boundaries: Coming from a non-tech background, she pushes herself to learn trending technologies like machine learning and augmented reality to empower her designs. Apart from digital products, she is interested in exploring designs at the intersection of software and hardware, online and offline.

No matter how interfaces changes, design is the key to make technologies accessible and serve people better.

When she's not designing, you can find her going to theaters for musicals & dramas, reading history, sociology & science fictions, and drawing.
