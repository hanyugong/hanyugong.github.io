Magic Laughter Bottle

Laughing is a common daily practice that has been regarded as beneficial to mental health. Some hospitals now offer laugh therapy as a complementary treatment to improve quality of life for people with chronic diseases.We hoped to discover a way to preserve this magical power and record every single happy second in our life. Therefore, we decided to design an audio interface that captures the characteristics of laughter in an innovative way.

Duration One week, Nov 2017
