Painting DJ

The rainbow has 7 basic colors, and music has 7 basic notes. How to visualize the music by color? Painting DJ is an interactive game based on Arduino. Users can tap the keyboard compose simple melodies, in the meanwhile, correspondent pigments will drop on a spinning plate and create different visual expressions.

Duration Apr - May, 2016
Team Hanyu Gong, Bo Han, Jiaqi Guo
