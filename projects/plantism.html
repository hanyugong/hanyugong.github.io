" Raise Your Plants Scientifically "

For many plant amateurs, how to maintain plants vitality is a challenge: They don't know when and how often they shall water the plants, or what temperature is the most habitable. Our design offers botanical greenhands planting tutorials and supervises living conditions of your plants. We use a sensor to detect the temperature, sunshine and humidity in the soil. Users can check the real-time status of their potted plants through a mobile app.
